ABOUT US

Who we are



Breaking bondages ministry is a church which started in 2010. We are powered by our Lord and saviour Jesus Christ. Our main aim is to preach the gospel and to deliver those are in bondage. BBM is a prophetic ministry where God delivers both prophecy and deliver those that are in captivity.



Our Story

Breaking Bondages Ministry was founded by Prophet Success Chinomona in 2010 with a very few people in attendance. The man of God gives an account of how God spoke to him whilst he had already departed the country in search for greener pastures in South Africa. At the point of calling the man of God returned to Zimbabwe and he started preaching the gospel.



The ministry started growing and our first church has been at a house in Unit O Chitungwiza the numbers started increasing and the we moved to Taita shopping center and now the church is located near Chitungwiza sign post near Seke/Dema Road. God has performed many miracles in Breaking Bondages Ministry from raising the dead, healing the sick, uniting the separated, breaking barriers for those who could not get the fruit of the womb and family deliverance.



Our vision is to do the will of He who started this project, Lord Jesus Himself. The bible tells us in the book of Acts how Jesus preached the gospel with power delivering those who are in bondage. We have a mandate to fufil His obligation and we are a point of contact for those who are in need of deliverance and prophetic. For those who are looking for intepretations for issues that they can not understand in their lives visit us.
