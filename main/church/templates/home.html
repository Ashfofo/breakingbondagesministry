Our Mission



Breaking Bondages Ministry is a prophetic and deliverance ministry with the main aim to deliver those who are in bondage,to enquire from God on matters that afflict the people of God. In the book of Samuel the children of Israel went to enquire God's opinion from a seer on matters that affected them. God has given us a seer Prophet Success to enquire on issues affecting our lives and be delivered by the power of God.
